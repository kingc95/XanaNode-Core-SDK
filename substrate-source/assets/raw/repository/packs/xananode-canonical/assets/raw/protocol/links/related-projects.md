# Related Projects and Lineage

XanaNode is influenced by a long lineage of work on hypertext, augmentation, and knowledge organization.

Important reference points include:

- Vannevar Bush - associative trails and Memex
- Douglas Engelbart - augmentation of human intellect
- Ted Nelson - hypertext, transclusion, Project Xanadu
- Tim Berners-Lee - World Wide Web and Semantic Web
- modern personal knowledge management systems
- knowledge graphs and graph databases
