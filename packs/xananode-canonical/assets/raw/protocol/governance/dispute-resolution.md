# Dispute Resolution

XanaNode treats disputes as knowledge, not merely failure.

A disputed claim should remain visible when appropriate, along with:

- who disputes it
- what evidence supports it
- what counterevidence challenges it
- what assumptions differ
- what sources are involved

Disputes can be represented with relationships such as:

- `contradicts`
- `contradicted_by`
- `disputes`
- `disputed_by`
- `alternative_to`
- `possible_match`
- `disputed_identity`

The goal is not instant consensus. The goal is navigable uncertainty.
