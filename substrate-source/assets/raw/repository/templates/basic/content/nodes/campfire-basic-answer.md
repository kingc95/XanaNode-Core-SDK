# A small fire starts with tinder, kindling, fuel, airflow, and a safe place.

A small campfire starts with dry tinder, kindling, fuel, airflow, and a safe place. Clear the area, keep water nearby, make a small tinder bundle, add kindling loosely enough for air to move, and only add larger fuel after the flame is stable.
